Hayao Miyazaki




Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Miyazaki?

84 years old.

When and where was he born?

January 5th,1941 in Tokyo, Japan.

How tall is he?

5’5 or 1.64 meters.

Does Miyazaki have family of his own?

Yes, he has been married to Akemi Ota, since October 1965, and has two sons, Goro and Keisuke Miyazaki.

Did he find Studio Ghibli by himself?

No, he co-founded Studio Ghibli with Isao Takahata, Toshio Suzuki, and Yasuyoshi Tokuma in 1985.

How did Miyazaki come up with Ghibli?

The studio was nicknamed after an Italian plane due to Miyazaki’s passion for aircrafts as well as the idea that the studio would “blow a new wind through the anime industry”.

Has he retired?

Several times, but it seems he doesn’t like retirement and come out to work on another film.

Does he use Computer Graphics?

He is skeptical about the whole thing because he commented once “Why do we need computers to do what human hands can do?” However, CGI has been used in very few and small sections in some films.

How many awards and nominations has he received?

89 awards won and 154 nominations.

Is he humble?

Yes, he does not like being praised, publicly or privately, and rarely accepts awards in person, especially in 2003 when Spirited Away won an Oscar for Best Animated feature. He refused to attend because as he told LA Times that he didn’t want to visit a country that was bombing Iraq.

Is he a feminist?

Yes, Miyazaki is also famous for his portrayal of women in his films and feminist themes.
